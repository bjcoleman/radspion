Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Radspion is operated by Moravian University for educational coursework. This policy describes what we collect when you sign in and use the site.

Information we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your Moravian Google account email address, display name, and Google account identifier. We store this to identify your agent profile, mission progress, and submissions.

How we use information

We use your account information to provide access to missions, record completion, and support course instruction. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Sharing

Course instructors and operators may view roster and mission data for classes you are enrolled in. We do not share your information with third parties except as required to operate Google sign-in or by law.

Retention

Data is retained for the academic term and reasonable period afterward for grading and records, then may be removed from active systems.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to your course instructor or Moravian University IT.
