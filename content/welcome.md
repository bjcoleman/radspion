Welcome to Radspion, a secret organization where agents carry out covert missions. This memo outlines the features of the agent dashboard, and it will be displayed on your dashboard until you complete your first mission.

## Your Codename

When you first signed in, you were assigned a provisional codename, which you can see in the upper-right corner of every page. Click on your codename to open your Agent Personnel File, where you can choose your own codename. Radspion Command reminds you that your codename is displayed publicly, and you should pick one that is appropriate and respectful.

## Field Activity

The [Field Activity](/activity) page (also linked in the page footer) shows a list of the top 10 agents and details about the various mission storylines. Radspion Command challenges you to become one of the Top Agents, to push yourself to discover a clearance code before anyone else does, or to complete a mission no one has finished yet.

## First Mission

Radspion Command encourages you to begin with [Basic Training](missions/basic-training), which is contained in the "Orientation" storyline, below.  This mission introduces you to the common activities that agents perform.
